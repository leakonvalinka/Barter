Community Guidelines

Welcome to Our Skills Exchange Community

Our platform connects people who want to exchange their skills and knowledge. These guidelines ensure a productive, respectful, and enriching experience for all members of our community.

These guidelines may be updated periodically to reflect our community's evolving needs. Members will be notified of significant changes.

1. Account Management

1.1 Profile Creation

  • Use a clear, professional profile picture or avatar
  • Provide accurate and current information about your skills
  • Maintain an up-to-date availability schedule

2. Skill Offers & Demands

2.1 Creating Skill Offers

  • Clearly describe your skills and what you can offer
  • Specify your available schedule for skill exchange
  • List any prerequisites or requirements
  • Keep your offers current and accurate

2.2 Posting Skill Demands

  • Clearly outline what skills you're looking for
  • Specify the urgency level from the provided options
  • Include your preferred schedule for the skill exchange
  • State any specific requirements or prerequisites

3. Communication Guidelines

3.1 Chat Etiquette

  • Use our platform's chat feature for all communications
  • Begin conversations with a professional greeting
  • Respond to messages within a reasonable timeframe
  • Maintain professional boundaries
  • End conversations courteously

4. Prohibited Activities

Violation of these prohibitions may result in immediate account suspension or termination.
  • Harassment or discriminatory behavior of any kind
  • Spam or excessive self-promotion
  • False or misleading information about skills
  • Sharing personal information without consent
  • Commercial solicitation outside platform guidelines
  • Multiple accounts for the same individual
  • Circumventing platform security measures

5. Dispute Resolution

5.1 Reporting Issues

  • Use the designated reporting tools within the platform
  • Provide detailed information about the incident
  • Report issues as soon as possible

5.2 Resolution Process

  • Moderators will review reports within 24 business hours (Vienna timezone)
  • Both parties will have the opportunity to present their case
  • Decisions will be communicated through the platform

6. Privacy and Data Protection

  • Personal information is protected under our Privacy Policy
  • Use only the platform's chat for communication
  • Keep your password secure and unique
  • Report any suspected security breaches immediately
Your privacy and security are our top priorities. Never share sensitive information through unofficial channels.
